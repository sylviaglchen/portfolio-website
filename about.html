About Me

- MY STORY -

As a product manager with a proven track record in delivering successful products, I am currently looking for opportunities to transition into the Product Design field.

I have always been interested in the tech business, how it works, and how it succeeds. My experience involves working in startups analyzing processes and efficiencies, as well as working as a product manager for a large-scale e-commerce platform.

During the pandemic, while I continue working as a product manager remotely with my teammates in different time zones, I completed a design bootcamp (600+ hours) and crafted my design skills, including user research, wireframing, high-fidelity design, prototyping, and user testing.

Having worked on different tech products, I understand how technology can positively affect people’s lives. As a UI/UX designer, my passion focuses on the users and understanding their real needs. My tech business background has shown me that as a designer, when I connect my products to the users’ needs, magic can occur.
